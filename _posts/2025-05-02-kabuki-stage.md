Kabuki [[Stage]]:

Dancing, 
Twirling, 
A story—
A play, 
Masks, costumes— 
Song and dance--
Half-Truths and [[Lies]]
The kabuki stage quakes—
Feet stomp--
Exposing
a sightless, soundless stage, 
Unseen,
Unheard,
Unknown,
Trapped, Caged--
an unending show--
Personally mine.
